Efficacious

Pronounciation:

/ˌef.ɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs/

Part of speech:

Adjective

Meaning:

Efficacious is a formal word used to describe something—often a treatment, medicine, or remedy—that has the power to produce a desired result or effect."
The use of efficacious for literary effect. "Similar approaches might be efficacious with respect to insect pests of agriculture."
