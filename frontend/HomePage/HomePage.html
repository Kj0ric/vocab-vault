Onomatopoeia

Pronounciation:

/ˌɒnəmatəˈpiːə/

Part of speech:

Noun

Meaning:

The formation of a word from a sound associated with what is named (e.g. cuckoo, sizzle). "A relatively large number of bird names arise by onomatopoeia."
The use of onomatopoeia for literary effect. "The language in her picture books is notable for its playfulness and onomatopoeia."
